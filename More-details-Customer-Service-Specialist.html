Customer service specialist ($16/hr)

As the customer service specialist, your job is to assist the customer with ordering, paying, and handing them the food. You will also need to package food together with the drivers. You will also be handling customers’ special requests, but don’t worry if something comes up and you can’t handle it, you can just call one of the two managers and they will be able to take it from you.
