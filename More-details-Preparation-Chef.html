Preparation Chef ($17/hr)

As the preparation chef, your job is to prepare all the materials needed for the stir fry chef and fryer chef. You will be chopping broccolis, cutting lettuce, wrapping Paper Fried Chicken, and many other things that need to be prepared. And just like the other 2 chef positions you will be expected to help with cleaning up and collaborating in the kitchen.
