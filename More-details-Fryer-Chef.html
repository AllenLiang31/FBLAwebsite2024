Fryer Chef ($17/hr)

As the fryer chef, your job is to operate the fryer with the help of a timer. You will be making things like Sweet and Sour Chicken, Egg Rolls, Egg Foo Young, Walnut Shrimp, and many other fried dishes. You will also be expected to help with cleaning up and collaborating with the stir fry chef and the preparation chef in the kitchen.
