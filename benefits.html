4 Benefits of Working at Rice King!





1. Inclusive Culture

At Rice King Express we value inclusion. We believe that everyone regardless of backgrounds or experience should feel like they are a part of our team. So we have a comprehensive training program that can teach you lots of beneficial customer service and packaging skills, so no one feels left out. We also understand that personal growth might vary and the paths you take in life will be different, so many of the skills you learn here can also be transferred to other fast food places which will make you feel ready and confident when it is time to say goodbye.






2. Perks and Amenities

At Rice King Express we offer many perks and amenities. We understand that working at a fast food place is hard and it’s definitely very tiring. So if you do decide to work here you will get free water and sodas. You will also be able to get a free meal every time your shift is over and if you want to buy food for your friends and family or you want to get more than one meal, they will be 50% off for you. So there will be no lack of perks and amenities if you work at Rice King Express.






3. Clear Career Pathways

At Rice King Express, we ensure your professional growth with well-defined career pathways. From day one, we will explain a clear understanding of the exciting opportunities that lie ahead. As mentioned previously the skills you learn here are not Rice King Express specific and limited to us. You will be able to translate them to many other fast food chains and this will open doors to various opportunities in the fast food industry.






4. Social Responsibility

At Rice King Express a key value we have is food quality. We serve food not only for the experience of our customers but also for the well-being of the community. We make sure to pick all the ingredients we use and ensure that it is from ethical and sustainable practices. If you are interested you can come with us to shop for ingredients and equipment every Sunday. This is a very interesting process in which you will be able to learn where restaurants shop and what our thought processes are when buying ingredients.
