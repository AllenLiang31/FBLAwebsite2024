Company Overview

Rice King Express is a veteran in the fast food industry of Utah with 20 years of operation. We mainly serve Chinese food specially catered to fit the average American’s taste pallets with foods like Sweet and Sour Chicken, Beef with Broccoli, or Ham Fried Rice. We offer dine-in, pick-up, and delivery services. Additionally, with the introduction of food delivery apps like Grubhub or Doordash they have made our goal of serving quality Chinese food even more efficient.








The story of Rice King Express

The owners of Rice King Express are Jingyong Liang and Kunying Yan, and they have quite the experiences of being in the culinary business. Their journey started working in different fast food businesses in New York. But after 3-4 years they moved to Utah and started their own business with many others, Fortune Buffet. The buffet didn’t run as planned, so they quit and left the management to the others. After a few years the entire business was closed, but the two of them already had plans. They decided to open their own restaurant, Rice King Express. And after 20 years Rice King Express is still thriving in its current location 3649 W 10400 S #106, South Jordan, Utah, 84095.
