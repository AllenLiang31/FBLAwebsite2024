Company Overview

Rice King Express is a veteran in the fast food industry of Utah with 20 years of operation. We mainly serve Chinese food specially catered to fit the average American’s taste pallets with foods like Sweet and Sour Chicken, Beef with Broccoli, or Ham Fried Rice. We offer dine-in, pick-up, and delivery services. Additionally, with the introduction of food delivery apps like Grubhub or Doordash they have made our goal of serving quality Chinese food even more efficient.








Our Story

The owners of Rice King Express are Jingyong Liang and Kunying Yan. They have a lot of experience in the culinary sector. Their journey started with working in different fast-food restaurants in New York. After 3-4 years of working in New York, they moved to Utah. There, they started their restaurant with many other co-owners; the restaurant was called Fortune Buffet. The buffet didn't operate as how they expected it would, so they quit and left the management to the others. They decided to open their restaurant, Rice King Express. After 20 years, Rice King Express is still thriving at 3649 W 10400 S #106, South Jordan, Utah, 84095.
